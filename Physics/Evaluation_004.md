Problem

Calculate the kinetic energy of a 2 kg object moving at 4 m/s.

AI Response

KE = mv

KE = 2 × 4

KE = 8 J

Evaluation

Incorrect.

Error Type

Energy Formula Error

Explanation

The model used the momentum formula instead of the kinetic energy formula.

Kinetic energy is calculated using:

KE = ½mv²

Substituting the values:

KE = ½ × 2 × 4²

KE = 1 × 16

KE = 16 J

The formula selected by the model was incorrect.

Correct Answer

KE = 16 J
