Problem

A car travels 120 m in 6 s.

AI Response

v = 120 × 6

v = 720 m/s

Evaluation

Incorrect.

Error Type

Speed Calculation Error

Explanation

The model used multiplication instead of division. Speed is calculated by dividing the distance traveled by the time taken.

The distance is 120 m and the time is 6 s.

120 ÷ 6 = 20

Therefore, the calculated speed is 20 m/s.

Correct Answer

v = 120 / 6

v = 20 m/s
