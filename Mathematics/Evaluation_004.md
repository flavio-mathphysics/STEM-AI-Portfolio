Problem

A rectangle has length 10 cm and width 4 cm. Find its area.

AI Response

Area = 10 + 4 = 14 cm²

Evaluation

Error Type

Explanation

The model added the length and width instead of multiplying them. The area of a rectangle is calculated as length × width.

10 × 4 = 40 cm²
